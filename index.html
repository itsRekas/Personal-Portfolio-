Hello!

My name is Reginald Kotey Appiah-Sekyere but you can call me Rekas. I am currently a student at New York University-Abu Dhabi. My academic focus is on Computer Science, with minors in Applied Mathematics and Interactive Media. I identify as Ghanaian and my preferred pronouns are He/Him.

In 2021, I completed my General Science studies at Adisadel College, where I honed my passion for photography. Over the course of my three-year journey there, I developed and cultivated my photography skills out of curiosity. During my final year, I had the privilege of serving as the Deputy Head Prefect, an experience that further enhanced my leadership abilities.

In September 2022, I began my journey at New York University-Abu Dhabi. Initially, I planned to study Electrical Engineering, but as time passed, I discovered a newfound passion for Computer Science and Interactive Media. Being part of this diverse community has been a valuable experience, helping me develop and refine my communication skills continuously. Moreover, through student assistantships and involvement in student interest groups, I've had the opportunity to expand my work experience and deepen my understanding of life's principles.
During my sophomore spring semester, I had the opportunity to study abroad at New York University-Paris. I got to immerse myself in the vibrant culture of France and delved into the intricacies of the French language. I also had the privilege of experiencing the rich history and dynamic lifestyle of the local community. Moreover, my time in Paris offered me the opportunity to gain insights into the professional landscape through various work experiences. Beyond the confines of Paris, I explored the diverse landscapes and cultures of neighboring countries, adding layers of depth to my understanding of European life. Collaborating with individuals from diverse cultural backgrounds further enhanced my learning experience.
Apart from my academic and professional pursuits, I find joy in capturing moments through photography and videography, often indulging in the creative process of editing for leisure. During my leisure time, I relish in the entertainment of video games and movies, and cherish opportunities to socialize and bond with friends outdoors. Furthermore, I have a deep appreciation for nature, particularly animals and vegetation, and often find solace in immersing myself in their presence. Music also holds a special place in my heart, with a diverse range of genres enriching my daily experiences.
